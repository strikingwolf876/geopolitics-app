# Own your fockin' notes. 🍴

This is **your** notebook. Every note is a Markdown file committed to **your** git
repo — no lock-in, no subscription, no one reading over your shoulder.

## How it works

- You're editing in **Sveltia CMS**, running entirely in your browser.
- Saving a note = a **git commit** to your notes repo. That's the whole backend.
- Delete this note whenever you like — then write your first real one.

## Tips

- Add **tags** to group notes.
- Accents and emoji just work: *caffè, però, città* — 🎉🔥🍀
- Installed this as an app? It opens straight into your notes (the reading view).

Now go fock around and find out. ✍️
